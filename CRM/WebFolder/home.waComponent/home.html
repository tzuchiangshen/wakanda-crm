Getting Started With Wakanda Leads
Leads
Leads are the individuals or representatives of organizations who show interest in your products or services.
Contacts
Contact are the people in a company with whom you communicate and interact in pursuit of a business opportunity.
Accounts
Accounts are companies or department within in a company, with which you make business dealings.
Activities
Activity are a list or register of tasks, events, and calls associated to the CRM records pertaining to different modules.
